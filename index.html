INTRODUCTION

- Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami, it has been published in Shūeisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since March 5, 2018.

- It has an anime adaptation produced by MAPPA which currently has 1 movie and 2 seasons:

  • Season 1: October 3, 2020 until March 26, 2021.
  • Season 2: July 6, 2023 through today as it is still airing.
  • Movie: May 27, 2022.

- The story revolves around Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of sorcery to save his friend from death. Yuji becomes a vessel for the spirit of Ryomen Sukuna, a powerful demon, and must fight curses to protect the living.


ARGUMENTAL ARCS

Cursed Childhood


It tells the story of Yuta Okkotsu, a 16-year-old boy, victim of the Special Grade Curse, Rika Orimoto, who was his childhood friend.


Introduction


It tells the story of Yuji Itadori, an average high school student with above-average physical ability.


Cursed Uterus


Two weeks after the test given by teacher Satoru Gojo, the freshmen are assigned a new mission involving a special class curse.


Cursed Training


With Itadori brought back to life, Satoru Gojo decides to hide him for a while and train him to become a true sorcerer.


Confrontation with Mahito


Itadori is assigned with first grade sorcerer and former employee, Kento Nanami to work on a mission involving special grade spirits. A strange curse called Mahito strikes and will cause Itadori deep pain.


Exchange Festival with Kyoto


After training hard and improving his skills, Itadori returns to his classmates before the start of the Kyoto Goodwill Event. However, the students of the Kyoto Technical College of Magic are on a mission to kill him during the team battles.


Origin of Obedience


Faced with a lack of staff, the first-year students of Tokyo Metropolitan Technical College of Magic are sent to investigate a series of strange disappearances at Megumi's old school, and this threat could probably involve her older sister.


Stellar Plasm Vessel


It tells the story of Satoru Gojo's youthful days with his classmates, Suguru Geto and Shoko Ieiri. They receive the mission to protect and escort a young girl who will be the recipient of Stellar Plasm for Mr. Tengen.
